# AusBathyTopo (Australia) 2024 — national 250 m bathy/topo compilation, full AU EEZ.
# One Float32 COG in a ~2.8 GB zip (..._MSL_cog.tif), EPSG:4326, NoData -32767, MSL.
# Values are elevation (seafloor negative) → no negate. ~250 m → z9 (one above GEBCO);
# gbr30 (z12) wins the GBR/Coral Sea overlap, AusBathyTopo fills the rest of AU waters.
# (The per-survey 2-10 m AusSeabed COGs are a separate, deferred custom-fetch job — they're
# served via the portal/WCS + a coverage DB, not a clean static urllist like CUDEM.)
